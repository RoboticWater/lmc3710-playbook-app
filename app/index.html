Foundation

An interactive questionnaire based off of the Atlanta Community Engagement Playbook to aid community associations in organizing and growing the foundations of their organizations.

Bring People In
Recruit new members and volunteers to build your strength and to engage as a more powerful collective.
Know Your Community
Conduct an analysis of your community. Determine the issues that matter to people and the assets already present that can be called on to address those issues.
Focus on Issues
Work with issues instead of problems. Issues bring people together into concrete areas of focus; issues are solvable and can be organized around.
Talk it Up
Share information about the process and your successes after each key action or event, and at the end of the process.
